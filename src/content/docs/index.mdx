---
title: Open Aviation Briefings
---

Instructor Briefings that you can re-use and improve for future pilots.

This project is a work in progress. I'm beginning with the Australian
Recreational Pilot License as I move through that training myself,
with plans to continue adding further briefings as more people get involved.

For non-technical information about Open Aviation Briefings see [Open Aviation Solution's overview of the briefings](https://openaviation.solutions/open-aviation-briefings/).
