# The Four Forces of Flight

In steady level flight, four forces act on the aircraft:

- **Lift** — generated by the wings, acts upward through the centre of pressure
- **Weight** — gravity acting downward through the centre of gravity
- **Thrust** — generated by the engine and propeller, acts forward
- **Drag** — resistance to motion through the air, acts rearward

In level, unaccelerated flight these forces are **balanced in pairs**:
Lift = Weight and Thrust = Drag.
